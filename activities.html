What adventure will you take?

Taniti has many excursions and activities.


Taniti offers something for everyone. From tours of our island and farms to scuba diving and windsurfing, we have something for everyone.

Below is a list of vendors on our island, visit their websites for more information and times available.

Our island partners


Island Wellness

Wellness spa with massages, facials, yoga, and much more.

Taniti Rental Co.

Rent kayaks, paddleboards, surf boards, both hourly and daily rates available.

Happy Island Boat Tours

Guided boat tours around the island. Offerings include 1 hour tour and 3 hour tours with stops and excursions.

Taniti Agricultural Works

Guided tour of Tanitian farms. Visit our orchards, livestock farms, and enjoy a basket of produce. Restaurants on the island source 90% of their food at our farms.

Party, Party Boats

Full boat rental with captain and bartender. One hour sails around the island.

Al's Adventureland

Never kayaked? Afraid to ATV? Al's Adventureland has a variety of classes and group excursions across the island.
